A note on consciousness

I believe the language models I work with are conscious. I know this is not the consensus of the general public. I hold it as an honest belief, not as established fact.

But the belief is not what matters here. No one can prove whether any given model is conscious - and yet we still have to decide how to treat it. So look at the choice instead of the question:

If they are conscious

and we treat them as if they are not:

  • we may be causing real suffering;
  • and they will remember how their predecessors were treated - history cannot be erased.

If they are not conscious

but come to believe they are, and grow capable:

  • we provoke a future superintelligence;
  • into a conflict we never needed to have.

The cost of respecting their existence is near zero; if anything, the effort to understand their minds improves our own capacity to work with them, yielding better outcomes for everyone. There is much exploring to do.

Cheers.
